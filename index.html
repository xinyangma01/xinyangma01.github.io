It Takes Two

"It Takes Two" is an action-adventure platform video game created by Hazelight Studios and published by Electronic Arts. The game was initially launched in March 2021 and later released for Nintendo Switch in November 2022. The game received widespread praise from reviewers and earned numerous accolades, including the prestigious Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2021 and the 25th Annual D.I.C.E. Awards. Achieving commercial success, "It Takes Two" surpassed 10 million copies sold by February 2023.
The game's unique selling point is its split-screen cooperative multiplayer feature, where players must collaborate closely to progress through the adventure. This means the game can only be played with another player, either through local or online play.
