About Me

My name is Xinyang Ma and I'm currently in my second semester at Seneca. I used to be a full-time jewelry designer and maker. I have my own business and it was going pretty well. But I always have a passion for programming and I wanted to give it a try. Plus, doing my own business were both mentally and physically demanding. However, I won't give up on jewelry-making completely. Actually I'm still doing it on the side. That's a sweet spot I'd like to keep. I'm enjoying programming right now. I especially have a passion for web dev. And I wish to become a web developer after graduation.
I chose this game because it's the best game I ever played, Immersive, enlightening and aesthetically pleasing. I played this gamw with my boyfriend. It didn't take much longer for us to clear the game. And we played it over and over again until we knew exactly what's going to happen next. But we just can't resist it. It challenges you with right amount of puzzles and joys. It's not so hard that you need to look up for tips and tricks and finally give up but it's not too easy that you feel like this game was designed for kindergarten kids. whenver I feel mentally exhausted or I feel like I could use some help to escape reality, I play this game. I like to immerse myself in the breakingtakingly beautiful scenes and interesting plots and just forget about everything for now. It's like a remedy to me.
